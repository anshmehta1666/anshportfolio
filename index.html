Rare Chemistry

Performance CGI Ad

The Challenge: Rare Chemistry needed scroll-stopping advertising creatives that could immediately capture attention on crowded social feeds while reinforcing the brand’s premium skincare positioning.

The Strategy: We designed a cinematic CGI concept that places the product within an unexpected luxury environment — transforming the skincare tube into a monumental object carried by a yacht. This surreal scale illusion instantly captures attention while associating the product with luxury, exclusivity, and high-end lifestyle aesthetics.

The Outcome: The final CGI motion creative delivered a highly distinctive visual hook optimized for short-form advertising platforms. The unique concept increased viewer retention and helped the brand stand out in competitive digital advertising environments.

Rainbags

CGI & Product Animation

The Challenge: Rainbags needed visually striking marketing creatives that could showcase their luggage in a memorable way across digital advertising and social media. Traditional product photography failed to communicate the scale, durability, and premium design of the suitcase.

The Strategy: We created cinematic CGI environments that place the suitcase into unexpected real-world scenes — from urban streets to large-scale architectural installations. These hyper-realistic compositions transform the product into the focal point while highlighting its design details and color variations.

The Outcome: The CGI-driven campaign produced highly engaging visuals that feel both imaginative and premium, helping Rainbags present their product in a distinctive way while improving attention and shareability across digital platforms.

CaseCartel

Brand Identity

The Challenge: CaseCartel targets a very specific audience — Apple users who care about design, aesthetics, and premium products. In a saturated phone case market, the brand needed an identity that instantly resonated with iPhone users while feeling modern and memorable.

The Strategy: We designed a brand identity inspired by Apple’s visual culture. By referencing recognizable elements like the iPhone’s Dynamic Island and creating a playful Bitmoji-style character, the identity feels familiar to Apple users while still being unique. The system balances minimal Apple-like design with a memorable character element that strengthens brand recall.

The Outcome: The new identity positioned CaseCartel as a lifestyle-driven iPhone accessory brand, making it more recognizable to Apple users while creating a flexible visual system that works across packaging, social media, and digital campaigns.

Aloha

Brand Identity

The Challenge: Aloha needed a distinctive brand identity that could stand out in the highly competitive beverage market while appealing to a younger, lifestyle-driven audience. The brand required visuals that felt fresh, vibrant, and instantly recognizable on store shelves and social media.

The Strategy: We developed a playful and energetic visual identity inspired by tropical flavors and summer culture. The custom logotype features soft organic curves to reflect freshness and approachability, while bright citrus color palettes and fruit illustrations highlight the natural ingredients of each flavor. The system was designed to work seamlessly across packaging, social media, and lifestyle campaigns.

The Outcome: The new identity positioned Aloha as a fun, modern beverage brand with strong shelf presence and social appeal. The vibrant visuals improved brand memorability and created a cohesive visual system for future product launches and marketing campaigns.

Botanica Skincare

Branding & Packaging

The Challenge: The organic skincare market is heavily saturated with brands communicating the same message — natural ingredients and botanical extracts. This makes it difficult for new brands to stand out or justify premium pricing.

The Strategy: Instead of positioning Botanica as simply another natural skincare brand, we reframed the brand around bio-available botanical science. The visual identity blends botanical softness with subtle clinical cues. Clean typography, refined packaging, and minimal color systems create a visual language that feels both scientific and luxurious.

The Outcome: The repositioning elevated Botanica into a science-driven skincare brand. The identity system now communicates performance and purity while positioning the brand within a premium skincare category.

Meadow & Melt

Brand Identity

Odyssey Outerwear

Brand & CGI Campaign

The Challenge: Odyssey created a jacket using aerogel insulation but struggled to convey the high-tech nature of the fabric through standard studio photography.
