Botanica Skincare

Branding & Strategy

The Challenge: The organic skincare market is heavily saturated with brands that all communicate the same message — natural ingredients and botanical extracts. This makes it difficult for new brands to stand out or justify premium pricing. The Strategy: Instead of positioning Botanica as simply another “natural skincare brand,” we built a strategy focused on bio-available botanical science. The goal was to communicate that the ingredients are not only natural, but also optimized for skin absorption and real results. The visual identity blends botanical softness with subtle clinical cues. Clean typography, minimal packaging, and refined color tones create a system that feels premium, trustworthy, and modern. The The Outcome: This repositioning transformed Botanica from a typical organic brand into a science-backed botanical skincare system, allowing the brand to confidently sit in a higher-value category and appeal to customers looking for both nature and performance.

Meadow & Melt

3D Dynamic Web

The Challenge: A private spaceflight startup needed a digital presence that matched their futuristic engineering. Traditional static sites failed to capture the scale and ambition of their orbital vehicles.

Our Solution: We built a fully immersive WebGL-powered dynamic website. Users can interactively rotate and explore 3D CAD-derived models of the spacecraft directly in their browser. By utilizing Three.js and custom shader materials, we maintained 60fps performance without sacrificing photorealism.

The Result: Average time-on-site increased by 310%, and investor lead generation form submissions doubled within the first month of launch.

Brand & CGI Campaign

The Objective: Odyssey created a jacket using aerogel insulation but struggled to convey the high-tech nature of the fabric through standard studio photography.

Creative Direction: We built a comprehensive campaign from the ground up. This included a stark, brutalist typography system to position the brand as "survival tech" rather than "fast fashion."

CGI Execution: Instead of a traditional lookbook, we created full CGI environments—snowstorms and hyper-realistic fabric simulations showing frost bouncing off the material. This resulted in a campaign that looked like a sci-fi film trailer, generating a 500% ROAS on day one.
